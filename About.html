Our Story

TekkyFutbol was founded to redefine small-sided competition. Every touch, every play, every moment — built around creative flow and passion for the game.

Structure

Our 15-week league puts 20 teams through the streets’ version of football — intense, fast, and unforgiving. No halftime. No fear. Just flow.

Mission

We celebrate creativity, culture, and competition. The mission is simple — build a platform where players showcase their style and control their narrative.

Movement

TekkyFutbol’s movement is inspired by FIFA Street’s Last Man Standing gameplay — a reminder that only the bold thrive when pressure rises.
